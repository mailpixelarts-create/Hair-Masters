============================================

HERO OPTION A — "The Profile Study"
A single editorial portrait of a woman with razor-sharp geometric bob, clean center part, glass finish hair catching soft directional light. Profile view, jawline exposed, neck elongated. Skin luminous, minimal makeup — just strong brow and nude lip. Wearing structured black wool blazer with sharp lapels. Background: seamless warm grey studio backdrop (18% grey). Hair is jet black with subtle blue undertones, reflecting light like liquid. Stray hairs deliberately styled — two or three fine strands across the temple. Expression: composed, eyes closed or half-lidded, complete stillness.
Camera: Hasselblad H6D-100c, 80mm f/2.2, shot at f/4, 1/125s, ISO 100
Film stock: Kodak Portra 160 (digital emulation)
Lighting: Single large softbox camera left at 45°, fill card camera right at -2 stops. Hair light: small gridded beauty dish directly above and slightly behind subject, creating rim light on hair surface only
Lens: 80mm medium format equivalent, shallow depth of field, background fall-off beginning at ear level
Crop: 16:9 widescreen, subject positioned right third, negative space left
Output: 3200x1800px, 300dpi, sRGB, TIFF/PSD
Style references: Peter Lindbergh monochrome editorial, but in muted color
