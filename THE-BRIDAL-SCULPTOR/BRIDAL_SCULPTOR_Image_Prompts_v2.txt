NOTES FOR IMAGE GENERATION
================================================================
- All prompts designed for 85mm–105mm beauty compression aesthetic
- Warm ivory-champagne color grading throughout
- Soft directional lighting preferred over flat lighting
- Hair must remain fully legible (not blurred into background)
- Premium editorial retouching expected
- Avoid: harsh shadows, oversaturation, cluttered backgrounds, stock photography feel
- Target aspect ratios: 3:4 (portrait), 4:5 (editorial), 16:9 (atmosphere)
- Each prompt can be adapted for specific model diversity as needed
