06 The Suite

Client Standards

Who This Is For

People who understand that hair is not an accessory — it is architecture. You want a look that is engineered, not improvised. Built to perform under pressure.

What to Expect

A consultation that goes beyond style preferences. We discuss structure, movement, maintenance, and the life your hair needs to live. Every detail is planned.

The Booking Process

Inquire with your vision. We schedule a structural consultation to assess feasibility and fit. Not every project is accepted — standards protect the work.

Timing & Prep

Structural work requires precision time. Complex builds may take 3–5 hours. Arrive with clean, dry hair. Come ready to discuss the architecture you want.
