THE DIRECTOR

Norman James

Norman James is a red carpet director whose command of the room is matched only by his precision behind the chair. With over a decade orchestrating celebrity transformations, editorial campaigns, and high-profile premieres, he brings a director's eye to every strand.

His aesthetic is cinematic glamour — hair that photographs like a film still, moves like silk in slow motion, and commands the frame from every angle. Norman doesn't just style hair; he directs the narrative of image.

From A-list award ceremonies to luxury brand campaigns, his work lives at the intersection of red carpet spectacle and editorial artistry. Every appointment is a production, every look a premiere.

THE APPROACH

FOUR PILLARS OF DIRECTION

01

COMMAND THE ROOM

Every consultation begins with presence. We read the room, understand the narrative, and take charge of the transformation with cinematic authority.

02

BUILD FOR CAMERA

Hair is designed under the assumption it will be photographed. Every angle, every highlight, every wave is constructed for the lens — not just the mirror.

03

SURFACE IS EVERYTHING

The surface tells the story. We obsess over texture, shine, movement, and silhouette — because the camera sees what the eye misses.

04

PRESSURE IS PRIVILEGE

Premiere deadlines, live television, campaign deadlines — we thrive under pressure. The stakes elevate the craft, and the craft rises to meet them.
