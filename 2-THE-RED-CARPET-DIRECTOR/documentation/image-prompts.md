# Image Prompts — THE RED CARPET DIRECTOR

World 08: Red Carpet Director
All prompts calibrated for AI generation (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion) or photographer briefs.

## 01 — HERO IMAGE (Primary)

**Prompt:**
A stunning Black woman in her late 20s, high-fashion editorial beauty portrait, red carpet premiere moment. Her hair is sculpted into an elaborate updo — architectural curls pinned with precision, each strand catching light like polished silk. Skin: luminous, dewy, flawless. Makeup: bold crimson lip, sharp winged eyeliner, sculpted cheekbones with golden highlight. She wears a single diamond ear cuff catching light. Expression: confident, knowing, slight smirk — she owns the room. Background: deep black void with subtle warm bokeh orbs (amber, gold) suggesting flashbulbs in the distance. She is lit from the left with a key light creating dramatic Rembrandt lighting — one side of her face in luminous clarity, the other in cinematic shadow. A rim light traces her jawline and the edge of her hair.

**Technical Specifications:**
- Camera: Hasselblad H6D-100c
- Lens: 85mm f/1.4 (converted to medium format equivalent ~65mm)
- Aperture: f/2.0 — shallow depth of field, creamy bokeh
- Film Stock: Kodak Portra 800 (digital emulation)
- Lighting: Single Profoto B10 key light at 45° camera-left, white reflector camera-right for fill, small LED rim light behind subject
- Color Temperature: 4800K — warm golden tones
- Color Grade: Rich cinematic palette — deep blacks, warm highlights, subtle teal in shadows
- Crop: 4:5 portrait (1600 × 2000px)
- Output: AVIF preferred, JPEG fallback, < 500KB target
- Resolution: 2400 × 3000px source

**Mood Keywords:** Dangerous elegance, controlled glamour, red carpet authority

## 02 — CAMPAIGN IMAGE (Runway Contact Sheet)

**Prompt:**
Three sequential editorial portraits of the same model, arranged as a horizontal triptych contact sheet. Left: profile view, hair swept into a chignon, dramatic side lighting. Center: three-quarter view, hair cascading in defined waves over one shoulder, soft butterfly lighting. Right: frontal gaze, hair pulled back tight with a single sculptural curl at the temple, hard ring light creating catchlights in eyes. All three: same model, same black backdrop, same crimson lip. Each frame numbered in small white type at bottom-left corner (001, 002, 003). Thin white borders between frames. Film grain visible.

**Technical Specifications:**
- Camera: Canon EOS R5
- Lens: 105mm f/1.4
- Aperture: f/2.8 — sharp subject, controlled fall-off
- Film Stock: Ilford Delta 400 (converted to color with split-toning)
- Lighting: Profoto D2 500 with beauty dish (center), gridded strip boxes (sides)
- Color Grade: High contrast, desaturated midtones, warm skin highlights
- Crop: 3:2 horizontal per frame, triptych layout (4800 × 1600px total)
- Output: WebP, < 400KB target
- Resolution: 5400 × 1800px source

**Mood Keywords:** Runway precision, editorial authority, contact sheet intimacy

## 03 — BEAUTY MACRO (Detail)

**Prompt:**
Extreme close-up beauty macro of an eye and brow area. The eyebrow is perfectly sculpted, each hair visible. Eyeshadow: smoky gold blending into deep espresso at the outer corner. Long, separated lashes coated in carbon-black mascara — individual lashes visible. The iris is deep brown with a ring of amber near the pupil, catching a single pinpoint of light. Skin texture is real — pores visible, no airbrushing. A single stray hair catches light near the temple. Background: pure black. The image feels intimate, like leaning in close at a vanity mirror.

**Technical Specifications:**
- Camera: Nikon Z9
- Lens: 105mm f/2.8 Macro
- Aperture: f/4.0 — enough depth for eye + brow, background falls to black
- Film Stock: Fujifilm Velvia 50 (for color richness)
- Lighting: Ring light directly in front, creating circular catchlight in iris, + small fill card below
- Color Temperature: 5500K — neutral daylight
- Color Grade: Rich, saturated, editorial precision
- Crop: 1:1 square (2000 × 2000px)
- Output: AVIF preferred, < 300KB target
- Resolution: 2400 × 2400px source

**Mood Keywords:** Intimacy, refinement, transformation through detail

## 04 — BACKSTAGE DIARY (Candid)

**Prompt:**
Backstage at a fashion show. A hairstylist's hands — male, mid-30s, tattooed forearm — are mid-motion, pinning a final curl into a model's updo. The model is seen from behind, her neck exposed, a diamond drop earring catching light. In the soft-focus background: a makeup artist with a palette, a rack of garments, warm vanity bulbs creating bokeh circles. The foreground is sharp — hands, pins, hair texture. The background dissolves into atmosphere. The moment feels stolen, unposed, real. Vanity mirror lights create a warm amber frame around the scene.

**Technical Specifications:**
- Camera: Sony A7R V
- Lens: 50mm f/1.2
- Aperture: f/1.4 — extremely shallow, hands sharp, everything else dreamlike
- Film Stock: Kodak Gold 200 (warm, nostalgic grain)
- Lighting: Available light — vanity mirror bulbs (3200K), no additional flash
- Color Temperature: Mixed — warm tungsten dominant with cool daylight spill from windows
- Color Grade: Warm, slightly lifted shadows, golden highlights, film grain overlay
- Crop: 4:5 portrait (1600 × 2000px)
- Output: JPEG, < 350KB target
- Resolution: 2400 × 3000px source

**Mood Keywords:** Insider access, craft in motion, human artistry

## 05 — MATERIAL STORY (Hair Texture)

**Prompt:**
Macro beauty shot of styled hair — a cascade of defined, glossy S-waves flowing diagonally across the frame from top-left to bottom-right. Each wave catches light differently: the crests are bright, almost white-gold; the troughs are deep espresso. Hair health is visible — no frizz, mirror-like shine, each strand group defined. A single gold hair pin is visible at the top of the frame, barely in focus. Background: matte black. The image is about texture, light, and the architecture of hairstyling.

**Technical Specifications:**
- Camera: Phase One IQ4 150MP
- Lens: 120mm f/4.0 Macro
- Aperture: f/5.6 — deep enough for wave structure, background pure black
- Film Stock: N/A — digital capture, clean
- Lighting: Two strip softboxes at 30° on either side, creating parallel highlights along wave crests
- Color Temperature: 5200K
- Color Grade: High contrast, no color cast, pure blacks, warm gold highlights
- Crop: 16:9 horizontal (2400 × 1350px)
- Output: AVIF, < 250KB target
- Resolution: 3200 × 1800px source

**Mood Keywords:** Couture craftsmanship, material obsession, luxury precision

## 06 — CINEMATIC FRAME (Atmospheric)

**Prompt:**
Wide cinematic shot. A woman stands at the end of a long, dark corridor — her silhouette framed by a distant doorway flooding warm golden light. She is in profile, mid-stride, one hand adjusting a strand of hair. The corridor walls are matte black with subtle texture. The floor is polished concrete reflecting her silhouette and the golden light. The depth of field is deep — both near and far are in focus. The image has a 2.39:1 widescreen aspect ratio with letterbox bars. Film grain is visible. The mood is suspenseful, like a frame from a fashion film — she is arriving, or departing, and we are watching.

**Technical Specifications:**
- Camera: ARRI ALEXA Mini LF
- Lens: Panavision Ultra Vista 40mm anamorphic
- Aperture: f/4.0 — deep focus, environmental storytelling
- Film Stock: ARRI LogC4 → Kodak 2383 print LUT
- Lighting: Single source — practical golden light from doorway, no additional fixtures
- Color Temperature: 3200K warm tungsten dominant
- Color Grade: Cinematic — lifted blacks, warm highlights, teal push in shadows, anamorphic lens flare
- Crop: 2.39:1 widescreen (2400 × 1004px)
- Output: WebP, < 400KB target
- Resolution: 3200 × 1338px source

**Mood Keywords:** Cinema, anticipation, fashion film narrative

## 07 — EDITORIAL SPREAD (Magazine Layout)

**Prompt:**
Double-page magazine spread layout. Left page: full-bleed portrait of a model looking directly into camera, chin slightly lifted, hair in a sculptural high ponytail with wrapped base. She wears a structured black blazer, nothing else visible. Right page: white space with large serif typography reading "THE DIRECTOR'S CUT" in black, with smaller body text below ( lorem ipsum acceptable ). A thin gold rule separates headline from body. A small credit line at the bottom right. The portrait bleeds to the page edge on three sides (top, left, bottom) with a 20mm white margin on the right where it meets the text page.

**Technical Specifications:**
- Camera: Hasselblad X2D 100C
- Lens: 90mm f/2.5
- Aperture: f/2.8 — sharp subject, soft background transition
- Film Stock: Fujifilm Pro 400H (soft, editorial)
- Lighting: Large octabox directly above camera (butterfly), white V-flat fill below
- Color Temperature: 5600K — clean daylight
- Color Grade: Neutral, editorial, skin tones prioritized
- Crop: 3:2 spread (4800 × 3200px total)
- Output: JPEG, < 500KB target
- Resolution: 5400 × 3600px source

**Mood Keywords:** Editorial confidence, magazine authority, typographic elegance
