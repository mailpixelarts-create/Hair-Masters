NORMAN

Norman James is a precision cutter who treats hair as architecture. Every cut begins as a blueprint — lines measured in degrees, weight distributed with structural intent, silhouette sculpted like concrete and steel.

Trained in the Bauhaus tradition of form following function, Norman approaches each client as a unique engineering challenge. Bone structure, growth patterns, hair density — all mapped before a single剪刀 touches the hair.

The result: cuts that hold their shape in motion. Geometry that breathes. Precision that feels effortless.

THE
APPROACH

01

Geometry First

Every cut begins with geometric analysis. Angles calculated, lines mapped, proportions measured. The blueprint precedes the blade.

02

Every Millimeter Matters

Tolerance measured in fractions. A shift of 0.3mm changes the silhouette entirely. Precision is not a goal — it is the standard.

03

Dry Tells Truth

Hair lies when wet. The final calibration always happens dry — the only state where weight, fall, and movement reveal the true cut.

04

Symmetry is Non-Negotiable

True symmetry is structural, not visual. Checked from every angle, in motion and at rest. The cut must be mathematically balanced.
