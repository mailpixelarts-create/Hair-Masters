# 2. WORLD SUMMARY — THE ALCHEMIST

## Core world

The Alchemist is the **high-craft transformation world**.

This is the stylist who turns damaged, flat, uneven, dull, brassy, unshaped, or visually tired hair into something richer, more dimensional, healthier-looking, more expensive, and more alive.

The world is not about generic salon makeovers.

It is about **transformation as craft**:

* tonal correction
* repair intelligence
* dimensional gloss
* expensive brunette / blonde refinement
* cut + finish synergy
* material beauty
* visible before / after elevation
* luxury restoration
* hair that looks re-authored, not just “done”

## The image system must communicate

* transformation authority
* technical sophistication
* luxury repair and correction
* healthy shine and dimensional finish
* hair as material
* high-end salon craft without salon cliché
* premium editorial beauty, not influencer beauty

# 4. GLOBAL CASTING RULES

## Model casting direction

The Alchemist should not feel like a one-face portfolio.

Use a **small but coherent cast** across the prompt set.

## Recommended casting mix for Project 01

* **2 Indian models**
* **1 Middle Eastern / West Asian model**
* **1 ethnically ambiguous brunette model**
* **1 fair-skinned blonde model**
* **1 deeper-skinned model with rich brunette / gloss transformation potential**

## Casting requirements

Models should feel:

* high-fashion but believable
* premium beauty campaign appropriate
* bone structure strong enough for beauty crops
* hair quality suitable for visible transformation work
* emotionally composed, not overly smiley
* modern, elegant, expensive

## Avoid

* influencer-looking faces
* obvious bridal glamour faces
* overly commercial stock-photo smiles
* beauty pageant styling
* generic “salon model” energy
* heavy expression acting

# 5. GLOBAL HAIR RULES

## Hair outcomes across the project should prioritize

* glossy dimensional brunette transformations
* corrective blonding / refined blonde toning
* rich copper / warm tonal correction if useful
* visible repair and finish improvement
* expensive blowout / polished movement
* strong root-to-mid-to-end integrity
* healthy ends
* premium shine
* softness with control

## Hair should never look

* crunchy
* overcurled
* dry
* fried
* over-teased
* over-hairsprayed
* bridal in shape
* pageant-like
* synthetic
* obviously AI-generated

# 6. GLOBAL MAKEUP + WARDROBE RULES

## Makeup

Makeup should support the hair and never dominate.

Use:

* luminous skin
* restrained complexion work
* soft sculpting
* neutral lip or muted rose / brown tones
* brushed brows
* subtle eye definition
* clean editorial beauty finish

Avoid:

* glitter
* dramatic lashes
* bridal sparkle
* Instagram glam contour
* heavy lipstick focus
* beauty looks that pull attention from the hair

## Wardrobe

Wardrobe should be minimal, premium, and subordinate to hair.

Use:

* black silk
* espresso knit
* ivory structured shirt
* charcoal tailoring
* stone slip or clean beauty-campaign drape
* minimal neckline garments that expose neck / collarbone / hair line

Avoid:

* loud prints
* sequins
* bridal gowns
* trend-heavy fashion styling
* chunky jewelry
* visible logos

---

# 7. GLOBAL LOCATION / SET RULES

## Primary set language for The Alchemist

Use one of these controlled environments:

### A. Luxury transformation studio

* neutral luxury salon-lab setting
* polished mirror surfaces
* stone, smoked glass, brushed metal, muted beige / charcoal palette
* controlled and uncluttered
* elevated but believable

### B. Beauty editorial studio

* seamless backdrop or soft architectural set
* clean controlled environment
* ideal for hero, detail, and high-fashion transformation portraits

### C. Process bench / formulation station

* premium bowls, brushes, gloss formulas, swatches, foils, sectioning clips, product textures
* should feel craft-led, not messy

### D. Quiet editorial atmosphere vignette

* reflective surfaces
* pigment / gloss references
* towels, combs, formula bowls, metallic trays, wet shine textures, luxury haircare objects

## Avoid environments that feel

* cheap salon
* cluttered back room
* influencer vanity room
* generic wedding suite
* brightly colored lifestyle interiors
* random home bathroom
* suburban salon décor

---

# 8. GLOBAL LIGHTING RULES

## Lighting for The Alchemist must show

* shine
* tone
* dimension
* softness
* hair health
* material depth

## Preferred lighting styles

### 1. Luxury beauty soft directional light

Use for hero portraits and transformation portraits.

* large soft source from side/front-side
* controlled shadow
* strong shine on mid-lengths and ends
* skin remains soft but not flat

### 2. Gloss highlight edge lighting

Use for detail shots and premium hero moments.

* subtle back edge or rim to catch reflective strands
* emphasize surface shine and movement

### 3. Neutral editorial daylight simulation

Use for process / craft / formulation imagery.

* clean daylight feel
* soft shadow
* color accuracy
* premium realism

### 4. Controlled spotlight for dark brunette shine

Use sparingly where hair depth needs to read luxuriously.

## Avoid lighting that creates

* blown highlights on hair
* muddy color
* flat overhead salon light
* obvious ring-light reflections
* hard beauty light that destroys hair softness
* yellow tungsten contamination unless deliberately used

---

# 9. GLOBAL CAMERA / FRAMING RULES

## Camera language

The Alchemist should feel like it was shot for a luxury beauty campaign with transformation intelligence.

## Preferred focal language

* **85mm / 105mm portrait lens feel** for hero and transformation portraits
* **50mm editorial portrait feel** for some mid-shots / movement portraits
* **100mm macro / beauty close-up feel** for detail shots
* **35–50mm documentary beauty feel** for process shots where environment matters

## Framing system

Use a controlled mix of:

### Hero framing

* vertical editorial beauty portrait
* horizontal hero crop with negative space for website headline
* 3/4 portrait with visible hair movement and tonal depth

### Gallery framing

* front 3/4
* back 3/4
* side profile
* seated beauty portrait with hair drape
* standing shoulder crop with movement
* after-focused transformation composition

### Detail framing

* nape-to-midlength crop
* side hairline + gloss crop
* root-to-end tonal transition close-up
* ends texture and health close-up
* crown / parting dimensional detail

### Process framing

* hands mixing formula
* brush applying gloss
* sectioning / saturation
* blow-dry finish detail
* post-treatment shine pass

---

# 10. GLOBAL COLOR / FILM / GRADING RULES

## Grading direction

The Alchemist should feel **rich, neutral-luxury, and expensive**, not trendy or hyper-saturated.

## Color grading goals

* accurate brunette depth
* premium blonde neutrality where relevant
* healthy skin
* warm-neutral luxury tonality
* preserved shadow detail
* glossy highlights without clipping
* no fake HDR sharpness

## Finish references

* luxury beauty campaign digital capture
* medium-format editorial feel
* polished but not plastic retouching
* optional ultra-subtle film grain in atmosphere images only
* hair shine must look photographic, not CGI

## Avoid

* orange skin
* grey dead skin
* oversaturated copper
* icy fake platinum unless conceptually required
* crunchy over-sharpening
* over-smoothed hair strands
* waxy skin retouching

Each pair should use the **same model, same framing, same wardrobe, same lighting, and same camera position** so the website slider reads as a true transformation.

# SLIDER PAIR 01 — BRUNETTE CORRECTION + GLOSS TRANSFORMATION

## SLIDER 01A — BEFORE

**Asset use:** Before/After slider
**Category:** Slider pair 1 / BEFORE
**Orientation:** Vertical portrait, exact framing match with 01B

### Prompt

Beauty portrait of an Indian model before a luxury brunette correction and gloss transformation, hair medium-long and visibly flatter, duller, slightly uneven in tone, less reflective, less shaped, but still realistic and not exaggeratedly damaged, center part, neutral expression, minimal black top, photographed in a premium beauty studio with soft neutral background, straight-on chest-up 3/4 beauty framing, soft directional light that remains flattering and consistent, the “before” should look believable and underwhelming rather than disastrous, hair slightly dry in finish and lacking dimension

extreme damage caricature, messy unbrushed hair, comedic before image, harsh salon light, dramatic sad expression, clutter, low quality

**Asset use:** Before/After slider
**Category:** Slider pair 1 / AFTER

Use the exact same Indian model, same pose, same framing, same wardrobe, same studio background, same lighting setup as the before image, but now after a luxury brunette correction and gloss transformation: dimensional espresso brunette with soft mocha variation, healthy shine, smoother cuticle, visibly richer tone, more polished shape through the lengths, subtle refined bend, stronger finish, expensive transformation result, chest-up 3/4 portrait, premium editorial retouching, believable but impressive elevation

# 20. EXECUTION NOTES FOR IMAGE GENERATION

## Important generation rule

Do **not** generate all images with the exact same model, exact same backdrop, or exact same pose language.
The world needs consistency, but it also needs enough variation to feel like a real transformation portfolio.

## Maintain continuity in these areas

* neutral-luxury grading
* premium beauty lighting
* healthy hair finish
* controlled styling
* no influencer / bridal contamination
* transformation-first framing
* expensive brunette / blonde / gloss / correction language

# 21. FINAL QUALITY CONTROL CHECKLIST

Before approving any generated image for The Alchemist, check:

## Hair quality

* Does the hair look healthier after transformation?
* Is the shine believable and premium?
* Are the ends clean?
* Is the tone dimensional rather than flat?
* Does the style feel controlled rather than overdone?

## Styling quality

* Is the makeup subordinate to the hair?
* Is the wardrobe minimal and premium?
* Does the model feel like luxury beauty editorial, not influencer content?

## Lighting quality

* Can you clearly read the gloss and tonal depth?
* Are highlights controlled?
* Is there enough contrast without killing softness?

## World fit

* Does the image feel like transformation craft?
* Does it belong in a high-end correction / gloss / restoration atelier?
* Would it sit naturally inside The Alchemist site without breaking the world?

## Rejection triggers

Reject the image if it feels:

* too bridal
* too influencer
* too commercial-stock
* too “generic salon”
* too synthetic / AI-plastic
* too overstyled
* too empty of visible transformation value
