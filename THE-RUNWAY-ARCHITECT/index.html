06 The Suite

The Studio Experience

Who This Is For

People who understand that hair is not an accessory — it is architecture. You don't want a "look." You want a structure — engineered to your bone density, your light conditions, the life your hair needs to live between appointments.

The Consultation

We don't ask "what style do you want?" We ask "what does your bone structure demand?" The consultation maps your cranial geometry, analyzes fiber resilience, studies how your hair moves when you walk into a room. This is not a conversation. It is a survey.

The Booking Process

Inquire with your vision. We schedule a structural consultation to assess feasibility and fit. Not every project is accepted — standards protect the work. A deposit secures your construction slot. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable.

Timing & Preparation

Structural work requires precision time. Editorial builds: 2–3 hours. Complex architectural constructions: 3–5 hours. Arrive with clean, dry, product-free hair. Come ready to discuss the architecture you want — and the architecture your hair can sustain.

"Hair is the architecture of the face. It is the first thing seen and the last thing forgotten. Every strand is a structural decision — and every decision has consequences."
