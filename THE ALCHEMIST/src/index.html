06 The Experience

Client Standards

Who This Is For

People who are not looking for a basic haircut appointment. You want a real change — someone with taste who can see your potential and author it.

What to Expect

A consultation that goes deeper than color charts. We discuss face, texture, maintenance, lifestyle, and the version of yourself you want to meet.

The Booking Process

Inquire with your transformation goal. We'll schedule a consultation to determine if this is the right fit. Not everyone is accepted — standards protect the work.

Timing & Prep

Transformations require time. Complex color work may take 3–5 hours. Arrive with clean, dry hair. Come ready to be honest about what you want.
